Primary Election Guide – Spring 2014

By: Staff | April 14, 2014 View as "Clean Read"
Tweet
Print

Karen Tam

This spring Raleigh and Wake County voters will pick candidates in several local races to send to the General Election in Novembers. Wake District Attorney Colon Willoughby retired recently, leaving that seat open for the first time in more than 20 years. Lorrin Freeman, the Wake County Clerk of Court, is running for DA, which also leaves her seat open. Both of the top jobs in Wake County's court system have a number of serious contenders vying for a place on the ballot this November. Also up in the primary is a race for the Republican nomination for Joe Bryan's seat on the County Commission. Bryan is running again for that seat against fellow Republican Jim Puryear. The winner this spring will go up against a Democratic challenger in the fall.

Get out and vote May 6!
